One line

The pitch

Gamified Lives is an RPG habit tracker that verifies you actually did the habit. Goals become quests, habits become XP, and six life domains (Health, Sleep, Wealth, Mind, Growth, Recovery) live in one app instead of six.

Fact sheet

Key facts

  • Founder: Kevin Castaneda (solo)
  • Platform: iOS first, Android planned
  • Pricing: Free forever tier · Premium $9.99/mo or $75.99/yr
  • Tech: React Native, Supabase, Anthropic Claude Haiku, OpenAI GPT Image 1.5, RevenueCat
  • Launched: 2026
  • Location: United States
What's different

Why people cover it

  • Real verification. Photo, HealthKit, NFC, timer — proof, not the honor system.
  • Phoenix Bonus. 2× XP for 24 hours after a streak break — rewards coming back, doesn't punish missing a day.
  • Life Domains. Absorbs Cal.AI / MacroFactor / Sleep Cycle / Rocket Money / Daylio / I Am Sober into one app.
  • Goal auto-progress. Log a $50 save — the "Save $5,000" goal moves by itself.
  • AI that isn't a gimmick. Voice coach calls, smart food scanning with macros, cross-domain insights.
