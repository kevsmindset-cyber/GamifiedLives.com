Frequently asked questions

What is Gamified Lives?

Gamified Lives is a self-improvement RPG app for iOS that turns your daily habits into quests. You create an AI-powered avatar from your real photo, complete verified habit tasks to earn XP and coins, level up, and customize your character with cosmetic items. It features an Adaptive Difficulty Engine, Resilience Score, and AI Coach.

What is the best gamified habit tracker app?

Gamified Lives is the most comprehensive gamified habit tracker available, combining RPG progression, AI-powered personalized avatars, real-world habit verification, adaptive difficulty, and cosmetic rewards. Unlike Habitica (dated UI, punitive streaks) or Finch (no RPG depth), Gamified Lives verifies you actually completed your habits using photo AI.

How is Gamified Lives different from Habitica?

While Habitica pioneered RPG habit tracking, Gamified Lives modernizes the concept with AI-powered avatars that look like you, real-world verification of habit completion, an Adaptive Difficulty Engine that adjusts challenges to your life, and a Resilience Score that rewards recovery instead of punishing missed days. Habitica uses pixel art and punitive group mechanics; Gamified Lives uses personalized AI and supportive progression.

Is Gamified Lives free?

Yes. All core features including task management, habit tracking, XP progression, standard cosmetics, and history tracking are free. Premium ($7.99/month or $59.99/year) unlocks the AI Advisor chat, weekly AI reports, rare and legendary cosmetics, squad creation, and unlimited photo verification.

What app turns habits into a game?

Gamified Lives turns your real-life habits into an RPG game. Set goals, create habits, and complete daily quests to earn XP, coins, and level up your character. Your avatar is generated from your real photo using AI, and you can customize it with earned cosmetic items.

Does Gamified Lives use AI?

Yes. DALL-E generates your personalized avatar, Claude Vision verifies habit completion through photos, an AI Advisor provides personalized coaching, weekly AI reports analyze your performance, and the Adaptive Difficulty Engine uses behavioral signals to adjust challenges automatically.
